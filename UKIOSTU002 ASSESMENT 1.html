About Our Photography Club

Our photography club meets every Friday after school. We welcome students of all skill levels!

What We Do:

  • Weekly photo walks around campus
  • Photography technique workshops
  • Photo editing sessions
  • Monthly photo competitions
  • Guest speaker presentations

How to Improve Your Photography:

  1. Learn the rule of thirds
  2. Practice taking photos daily
  3. Study lighting conditions
  4. Experiment with different angles
  5. Edit your photos thoughtfully
  6. Share your work for feedback
